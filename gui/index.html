Rules Summary

Sholo Guti is a traditional 16-piece alignment game. Players take turns moving a piece to an adjacent empty node along the board lines.

Capturing: Jump over an opponent's piece into an empty node directly behind it to capture it. Multiple captures are allowed in a single turn if structurally possible!
